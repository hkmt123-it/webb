The Challenge of Training Air Traffic Controllers

Traditional ATC training relies heavily on physical simulators and in-person instruction, making it costly, geographically limited, and difficult to scale. As air traffic demand grows globally, the need for a flexible, standardized, and accessible training solution has never been greater.

"The global shortage of qualified air traffic controllers is one of the most pressing challenges facing aviation today."

— ICAO Global Aviation Safety Plan

What is the ATC Online Training System?

A modular, web-based Learning Management System (LMS) built specifically for air traffic controller training — combining theory, simulation, and assessment in one platform.
